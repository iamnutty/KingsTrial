
Chess inspired game with fog of war and neutral pieces and point based promotion. The player pieces can also be re-spawned post capture in specific re-spawn points. 

Board is 20 rows x 8 columns can be notated as A1 to H20. Think of it like a chess board thats extended . 
We can mark the ranks 1,2,3 slightly whited out and 24,25 & 26 slightly greyed out . No piece can move into these ranks . 

Both sides start with 4 pawns and 1 king
White : Pawns in C2, D2, E2, F2 & King in E1
Black : Pawns in  C19, D19, E19, F19 & King in D20
Neutral : Pawns in D14, E14, D7, E7.  

**Game loop :** 

Will have 2 player and 2 neutral turns which will respond to moves from either player . Player Order : White , White Neutral response , Black, Black Neutral response and then back to white 
When White Neutral turn, all neutral pieces will be evaluated as black pieces with blacks turn and all Player pieces will be considered as white pieces  
When Black Neutral turn, all neutral pieces will be evaluated as white pieces with whites turn and all Player pieces will be considered as black pieces  
In case there are no neutral pieces active or any player pieces within active neutral turn board, then return all available neutral pieces should move back to the starting squares. 
The above neutral treatment is how the board position should be loaded into the chess engine for evaluation and move suggestions

The game will have an integrated point + timer system. 
Time control : Each player including neutrals will have a time increment per move (5 sec default) and will start with 1 minute on clock at the start. 
When each player makes a move they will earn 1 point . Additionally any pieces captured give points same as chess. ( Pawn = 1, Knight = 3, Bishop = 3 etc. ). 
Promoting pieces can be done by spending points on pieces. All pieces can be promoted.  
If a players time runs out, control will shift to the next player in the game loop . They will loose 1 point and when its again their turn to move, they will still get time increment as if they had made the move so they can make a move when their turn comes next .  

**Playing variations from chess.** 

	PAWN
		The Players (Not neutrals) pawns can move like kings in all direction. 
	No En pass-ant / promotion of piece as it hits the last rank of an active board 
	No 2 step moves in beginning 

	KING 
		No castling 

	Rook 
		No castling 

	Promotion :
		All pieces except the King can be converted/ promoted to all other pieces except a King. 
		Each of this "promotion" is considered a legal move 
		Players can spend the points accrued to change / promote a piece
		Cost of promotion is the points of piece + 1. ( For example Knight will be 3+1 = 4, Rook will be 5 +1 = 6).

	Respawn : 
		Player pieces can respawn . 
		Neutral pieces cannot respawn after being captured . 
		Player pieces captured can be respawned next to the respective king in a subsequent turn by spending the same number of points as they are worth in any of the empty squares adjascent to the King . It will be treated as a move.
		In case a king is captured, then the opponent receives 20 points. If the captured King is that of a player (Not neutral), All the captured pieces of the captured Kings player are considered as captured and removed from the board. In the next turn of that player the King is automatically respawned in the original respawn square (White : e4 , Black : d23) provided that its empty. If not empty then that turn is considered as "no valid move possible" for that player and the clock can run out and the game moves on based on the other rules.  



**Winning Criteria :**
			You do not win if the opponents time runs out 
			The total legal move count is fixed (default is 49) at which point the result is calculated based on points of the players. If equal a draw is declared. 
			If a white king happens to reach 22nd rank or the black king happens to reach 5th rank the game ends and then that player wins  
			Killing an opponents King gives you 20 points 
			When a king dies, he has to spawn at the respective starting position and all active pieces are taken off the board. The entire team has to be re-spawned by spending points on their re-spawn similar to when they respawn post capture  

**Neutrals :**
Neutrals will be controlled by an AI which will be fed a chess position. Preferably Stock fish , based on the level chosen for the game. 
The current chess position to be fed to the AI for move options will be generated by the active board function. 
The AI will be held to the same 5 sec per move shot clock in its turns. If there is a valid active "Neutral"  board, then the engine will be forced to make a move within the shot clock time. 

**Active Boards**  

Each active board will be a 8x8 portion of the overall game board.  This is essentially the board that the current player whose turn it is has visibility to . Rest of the board is dulled by fog of war not revealing the position of the pieces. 

Each player in the game loop ( Including neutrals ) will see the position from an "active board" lens. The active board will be generated along with the colour of pieces etc. to be fed into the chess engine in the acceptable format. 

White Player Board : This will be the board that the white player will see.  

Black Player Board : This will be the board that the black player will see. 

White Response Neutral Board :  This is the board that will be used for evaluating the neutral pieces move post white players move. All neutral pieces will be treated as black pieces and all (both) player pieces will be treated as white. 

Black Response Neutral Board : This is the board that will be used for evaluating the neutral pieces move post Black players move. All neutral pieces will be treated as white pieces and all (both) players pieces will be treated as black . 

Logic to determine active board :  (The below algorithm is what am thinking but if there is a better way to handle a balanced neutral turn visibility , am open to suggestions as we code)

White Player turn : 
The active sub board will be 8x8. The board will be such that the King is in the 4th rank of the sub board. 

White Response Neutral Board : Calculate each neutrals board range (min and max board rank assuming that it is in the 5th rank of the respective board) . Check if there are any play pieces within that sub board range. Only consider the options that have player pieces in them. Weight each 5th rank board option with the points of the piece used and choose the candidate for the sub board 5th rank based on the highest weight . If there are no player pieces in any of the response options then the board rank range will be 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16 , 17.  All non captured neutral pieces will return to their original positions

Black Player turn : 
The active sub board will be 8x8. The board will be such that the King is in the 5th rank of the sub board. 

Black Response Neutral Board : Calculate each neutrals board range (min and max board rank assuming that it is in the 4th rank of the respective board) . Check if there are any play pieces within that sub board range. Only consider the options that have player pieces in them. Weight each 4th rank board option with the points of the piece used and choose the candidate for the sub board 5th rank based on the highest weight . If there are no player pieces in any of the response options then the board rank range will be 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16 , 17. All non captured neutral pieces will return to their original position

Game play effects 
Mouse will be the primary input mode . Only the pieces of the current player can be chosen. Only the active board in the current phase will be without the fog of war and clicks within that area will be recognised. Single click of a piece selects or if already selected it can deselect. After selecting a piece if a valid square or a valid piece capture based on the type of piece is selected, the move is done. If a piece is double clicked and there are enough points with the active player a promotion dialog box appears above the piece with possible options ( based on points required for a promotion and points available with the player such that their point count does not become negative). If the player clicks outside the box without selecting any piece type for promotion then the dialog box disappears and the piece remains in selected state . If a player clicks another of their own piece even when one is already selected, move the selection to the new piece. These are the normal expectations of any chess game , in case any edge case is missed, please check with me on them so that the game play is as smooth and similar to typical computer chess / checkers mouse game play flows. 

If any player wins or the move count is complete in a draw, please display that in the same section which displays the game status ( move number, player turn , phase etc. )
