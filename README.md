# Kings Trial: The Origin Story

**The crown isn't given; it’s earned in blood and strategy. Survive the trial, claim the throne.**

Available on itch.io: [https://gamedabler.itch.io/kingstrial](https://gamedabler.itch.io/kingstrial)

---

## The Vision
*Kings Trial* reimagines the classic rules of chess as a brutal, high-stakes battle for survival. This is the origin story of the King—before the checkmate, there was the struggle.

In this MOBA-inspired chess variant, opening theory takes a backseat to tactical adaptability. With an unpredictable, AI-driven Neutral force and a shrinking battlefield, every decision matters.

## Gameplay Overview
- **Growth & Evolution:** Start from humble beginnings and use points earned through movement and captures to promote your pieces and raise an army.
- **The Arena:** The board is not a static grid; it collapses every 15 moves. Don't get caught in the dead zones!
- **Adaptive AI:** Powered by Stockfish, the AI is tuned to challenge your strategy dynamically, forcing you to think on your feet.
- **Winning Conditions:** Reach the far end of the board with your King faster than your opponent, or outscore them within 75 moves.

## Developer Log & Current Status
*Kings Trial* is built entirely in **Pygame**, focused on bridging the gap between classic chess and high-chaos strategic warfare.

**Current Status:**
- **Visuals:** Leveraging traditional sprites for a clean, familiar aesthetic for online chess players.
- **Music:** Original tracks are included.
- **Networking:** Private game hosting for online play with friends is actively in development.
- **Customization:** Highly customizable themes (Music, Sprite/Colour sets) via JSON files.
